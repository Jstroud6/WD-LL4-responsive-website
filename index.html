Eco-Friendly Travel Tips

1. Bring a reusable water bottle and shopping bag.
2. Use public transportation, bike, or walk when possible.
3. Choose eco-friendly accommodations and tours.
4. Respect local wildlife and natural habitats.
5. Reduce waste by avoiding single-use plastics.
